Chatterpast

Tolerant Futures through Ancient Identities

Use our magical messaging app to explore life in Iron Age and Roman Britannia with Enica and her friends. Chat directly with them and discover their unique stories from across Scotland, England and Wales. Question them about their lives and experiences and reflect on how these relate to the present.

Chat with the past

Make it your own

Are you an educator addressing Iron Age and Roman Heritage? You can use this story in your teaching! We’ve developed this project as a reusable set of open educational resources. This includes the characters, their backstories, the story script, illustrations and the source code of the chat story. Check out the Teachers’ Guide for more context and ways to adapt the story to your needs.
